Salmon florentine

A fresh new take on the typical florentine dish

comes together in just 30 minutes. Chock full of spinach, garlic, and mild spices, and topped off with a creamy, buttery, delicious cream sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Minced garlic
  • Chicken broth
  • Baby spinach
  • Heavy cream
  • Cream cheese

Steps:

  1. 6 sheets sushi seaweed aka nori
  2. 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  3. 1/2 lb sushi-grade raw salmon or desired raw fish of choice
  4. 4 oz cream cheese sliced into strips
  5. 1 avocado sliced
  6. soy sauce for serving
